SmartKidTimer – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-06

SmartKidTimer is a parental time‑management app. We do not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for the app to function on your device. All timer data and profiles are stored locally on your device.

Data We Collect

  • Local app data: profiles, preferences, timers, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No account: SmartKidTimer does not require an account or login.
  • No analytics/tracking: We do not use third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • Media access (optional): If you choose a custom avatar/photo, the app may request Photos access to pick an image. Images remain on your device.

How We Use Data

  • To save your preferences and timers locally.
  • To display optional media you select.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

SmartKidTimer is designed for families. We do not collect information from children. Parents/guardians should supervise usage.

Security

iOS provides device‑level protections (passcode, Face ID/Touch ID). We rely on Apple’s platform security for local data protection.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the app to remove local data.
  • You can revoke Photos permission in iOS Settings at any time.

International Transfers

No data is transferred to our servers; we do not operate backend services for this app.
